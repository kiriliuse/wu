Keeping track of money in an often conservative way.
Main benefit is with protecting creditors (banks, suppliers, state).
### Types of Accounting

- Managerial

- Financial

		- investors
		- banks
		- b2b partners
- Auditing
	- reviewing the accuracy of financial reports
